Process

To understand more about violent games, you need to read more information about the reasons, effects and the solutions for playing violent games of young people. You can find information on the websites, magazines, newspaper, videos etc. You have to pick up appropriate information to support your own ideas and make a logical presentation. The presentation should last about 5-7 minutes.
